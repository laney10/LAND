ExCellBio 293 Serum-Free media

Empowering Scalable Biopharmaceutical Production

In the fast-growing field of protein expression cell and gene therapy, 293 Serum-Free Medium​ has become an indispensable solution for high-performance suspension cell culture. Engineered to eliminate reliance on animal-derived components, this advanced medium ensures consistent cell expansion, enhances viral vector yields, and meets stringent regulatory requirements—critical for modern therapeutic development.

Animal origin-free, chemically-defined formulation Sustains high viability in transfected suspension 293 cells Supports high-density growth of multiple 293 cell lines Enables efficient transfection and high-level protein expression GMP-grade
