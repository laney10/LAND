ExCellBio MSC serum-free culture medium

Specifically designed for human mesenchymal stem cell (MSC) culture, supports efficient expansion of MSCs from sources like umbilical cord, bone marrow, and adipose tissue.

MSC Serum free medium is specially optimized for the culture and expansion of human mesenchymal stem cells (hMSCs), under completely serum-free and xeno-free conditions. MSC Medium supports the long-term cell culture and expansion of MSCs for multiple passages, while maintaining their various diﬀerentiation.
