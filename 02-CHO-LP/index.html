ExCellBio CHO Serum-Free media

Revolutionizing Biopharmaceutical Production

In the fast-evolving field of biopharmaceuticals, CHO (Chinese Hamster Ovary) serum-free media has emerged as a game-changing solution for scalable, high-performance cell culture. Designed to eliminate reliance on animal-derived serum, this advanced media ensures consistent cell growth, enhances protein yield, and simplifies regulatory compliance—critical advantages for modern therapeutic development.

OptiVitro® CHO Serum-free Basal media series is a state-of-the-art, animal-free, protein-free, and chemicallydefined media that is specifically designed for the high-density culture of CHO-K1, CHO-DG44, and CHO-S etc.

Initial Seeding Density:

0.6–1.0 × 10⁶ cells/mL. Seed cells should be in the logarithmic growth phase with a viability of no less than 95%.


Culture Conditions:

Temperature: 37°C
Humidity: 80% relative humidity
CO₂: 5%


Agitation:

120–150 rpm, 50mm (reduce speed appropriately for larger volumes).


Optional Passaging:

If conditions allow, pass cells once or more in the three basal media prior to inoculation. Alternatively, direct inoculation into the test media can be conducted, in which case centrifugation and resuspension is recommended to minimize the influence of previous media on screening results.
